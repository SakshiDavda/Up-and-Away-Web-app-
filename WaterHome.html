Water Adventures

With numerous rivers running through the country and the massive ocean below us, there is no dearth of exciting travel opportunities for those enchanted by the soothing blue waters. From the enchanting backwaters, the crystal clear mountain rivers to the calming seas and the breathtaking ocean, explore them all on a ‘hatke’ holiday experience. For a truly spectacular summer plan a trip to one of the following destinations for insane water adventures in India.


FLY BOARDING

Flyboarding is a water-based adventure activity that is relatively new in India. It essentially consists of a skateboard-like structure that is powered by a machine that thrusts water at it with massive force.

The force propels you into the air and you are literally floating, powered by the water’s thrust. Places such as Goa and Gokarna offer flyboarding activities.

Places to Experience Flyboarding: Goa

JET SKIING

Glide through the unending waters of the Arabian sea, with the wind in your hair and the warm golden sunshine. Jet skiing is one of the most enjoyable and popular watersports in Goa and a must-do irrespective of your comfort level.

Best Season: October to May

SCUBA DIVING

Away from the mainland of India, explore the exotic underwater world and untouched beauty of the beautiful Andaman Islands. With azure coloured waters in every direction, Havelock Island is one of the best sites to scuba dive in the country. And if you are not into diving then explore the world with a Sea Walker helmet or snorkelling gear to watch the corals and colourful fish. And definitely indulge in adventure sports

Places to Experience Scuba diving: Andaman, Goa.

WINDSURFING

Extremely thrilling and exciting, windsurfing is a unique blend of surfing and sailing on the waves of Arabian Sea. Ride the waters along the changing wind direction for an amazing time. The feeling of pride when one directs the board is unmatchable. This is one of the most amazing water activities.

Places to Experience Windsurfing: Goa, Pune, Mumbai.

SEA WALKING

Sea Walking first began at Andaman and Nicobar Islands in India. Ofcourse, these islands are a bliss to experience both on land and underwater. Tempting blue beaches, crystal white sandy shores, pristine surroundings, vivacious marine life and the coral reefs give an unforgettable experience in Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

Places to Experience Sea walking: Andaman and nicobar island, North bay island, Elephant beach.

PARA SAILING

Fly off into the sky as a speedboat tugs you along and enjoy the spectacular view from up above. Parasailing is an extremely popular water sport, one of the easiest and most enjoyable water activities to indulge in.

Best Season: October to March(Goa)

WAKE BOARDING

Quite similar to Water Skiing, only with a single board instead of two, this sport requires lesser focus on balance. A great fun, this one is a must try if you are a water sport aficionado. Fighting with the refreshing water to keep yourself in balance and control makes this one of the most adventurous water sports.

Best Season: October to May

KITE SURFING

In mood for trying some kitesurfing? Well, you can surely do it if you’re an experienced person in this sport. If not, try getting some lessons first to help you have a fun time in those splashing waves here.

Best Season: January to March

CRUISE SAIL

Observe a new culture, explore Mughal-style gardens, visit a sleepy village and connect with nature, whilst gliding through quiet waterways and glimpse places off the beaten track. Travel from Kolkata on the Hugli River as it twists and turns between banks lined with jute fields and mango orchards. See the countryside of Mayapur, Nawab’s great Hazarduari Palace, moor at the delightful village of Baranagar and a lot more on this cruise, ideal for those wanting a truly unique experience.

Places to Experience Cruise sail : Andaman, Goa, Kerala

BANANA BOAT

Enjoy the thrilling ride with your friends on this giant banana shaped boat as your move ahead against the ocean waves avoiding being flipped over. This one is nothing if not fun and is amongst the easiest watersports in Goa.

Best Season: October to March(Goa)

RIVER RAFTING

Feel the rush of wind in your hair alternating with the quiet breeze of the river. One of the most popular watersport is white water rafting. Enjoy the ride on the River as you are surrounded by beautiful lush vegetation, trees and have a trip that your will not forget. This might be among the best watersports!

Places to Experience River rafting : Rishikesh, Goa, Himachal pradesh.

KAYAKING

More of a calm, exploration sport, kayaking is the perfect water sport activity for those who want to spend the afternoon paddling along the backwaters. If the weather allows, it could be amongst the most fun watersports during monsoon season.

Best Season: October to May

Places to Experience Kayaking : Kerala, Goa.

SNORKELING

Submerge yourself beneath the underwater world and get enchanted by the beautiful fish like batfish, angelfish, barracudas, snappers, lionfish, world war two wrecks and other underwater wonders. It is amongst the easiest watersports in India one could indulge in.

Best Season: October to March

Places to Experience Snorkeling : Andaman, Goa.

WATER ZORBING

If you wish to try watersports in beaches, this one will surely give you a unique experience. A minimum of two persons can be accommodated in the orb but it can be best enjoyed solo as you tumble across the waves in a giant ball.

Best Season: Summer

RINGO RIDE

If you haven’t tried riding a circular tube being pulled by a boat all the way, this is surely the time to do so. While eagerly waiting for the splashing waves to come back and forth, the main twist comes at the end of this ride where the tube tumbles upwards and you’re left floating in the waves.

Best Season: October
